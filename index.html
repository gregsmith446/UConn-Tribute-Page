Key Achievements

  • The 2014 title was the school's fourth title in just 15 years.
  • This was the first national title for Head Coach Kevin Ollie in his second season in the position.
  • UConn became the first #7 seed to win the NCAA Championship.
  • The UConn Women's team also took home their respective title in 2014. UConn is the only school to list the double acheivement and have done so twice; in 2004 and 2014.
  • UConn is undefeated in the state of Texas in the Final Four (6–0).
  • UConn has never lost a title game (4-0).
  • Shabazz Napier, along with UConn teammates Niels Giffey and Tyler Olander, are the only Division I men's players in history to have won national championships as both freshmen and seniors.
